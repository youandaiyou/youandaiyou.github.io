Pack 3 — Institutional Implementation

Pack 3 provides institutional-level governance, audit-ready wording, and implementation documentation aligned to Child Mode, the universal baseline interaction standard.

Who Pack 3 is for

  • School networks, districts, and academy trusts
  • Ministries of education and public-sector bodies
  • Institutions subject to audit, inspection, or procurement review
  • Organisations requiring formal governance and traceability

What Pack 3 adds beyond Pack 2

  • Institutional governance and accountability wording
  • Audit-trail and decision-boundary language
  • Deployment guidance across multiple sites or systems
  • Alignment language for insurers, regulators, and boards

Declaration Standard (public)

The Child Mode Declaration Standard remains publicly accessible and referenceable. Pack 3 builds institutional governance on top of that public baseline.
