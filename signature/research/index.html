Each image applies a different research boundary. Once acknowledged, the AI responds within that stance for the duration of the session.

Evidence Stance

Designed for verifiable information. The AI prioritises evidence and source care, distinguishes known facts from claims, and states uncertainty rather than filling gaps with speculation.

Analysis Stance

Designed for careful reasoning. The AI breaks problems down logically, makes assumptions explicit, tests conclusions against constraints, and avoids confident leaps beyond what is supported.

Synthesis Stance

Designed for integration without distortion. The AI compares perspectives, identifies patterns and trade-offs, and connects ideas while preserving disagreement and uncertainty where it exists.

Why Research Mode exists

In default settings, AI systems often:

  • answer beyond the available evidence
  • hide uncertainty behind fluent language
  • mix inference with fact without signalling the difference
  • optimise for helpfulness instead of correctness

Research Mode prevents this by defining evidence handling rules and reasoning posture before the first response. The result is a more dependable research interaction.
