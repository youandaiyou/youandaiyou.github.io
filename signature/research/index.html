What you receive

You receive three AI Signature™ image files for Research Mode.
Each image applies a slightly different research stance, allowing the conversation to stay precise, grounded, and under your control.

Research Mode offers three clear ways an AI can respond when accuracy, evidence, and clarity matter.
Each stance adjusts how information is handled so the interaction stays focused and dependable.

Evidence Stance

This stance prioritizes verifiable information.
Responses are grounded in evidence, sources are treated carefully, and uncertainty is acknowledged rather than filled with speculation.

Analysis Stance

This stance supports careful examination of ideas.
Information is broken down logically, assumptions are made explicit, and conclusions follow clearly from what is known.

Synthesis Stance

This stance helps bring information together.
Multiple perspectives are considered, patterns are identified, and connections are made without forcing a single viewpoint.
