A licensed interaction framework for writers who want AI support without losing authorship.

Author Mode — AI Signature™ defines how an AI is allowed to respond when you are writing. It governs tone, authority, and scope so that the work remains yours — not the AI’s.

This is not a prompt and not a content generator. It is a deliberate interaction stance designed to prevent voice drift, overreach, and unintended co-authorship.

Author Mode operates within the always-on AI Frame™, ensuring consistent governance across sessions and platforms.

Each image establishes a different authorship boundary at the start of a conversation. Once acknowledged, the AI responds within that stance for the duration of the session.

Writing Mode

Supports drafting and expression while preserving your voice. Responses focus on clarity, flow, and articulation without introducing original content or stylistic takeover.

Revision Mode

Supports refinement and editing. Suggestions focus on structure, wording, and coherence while respecting existing intent, tone, and authorship.

Development Mode

Supports longer-form and conceptual work. The AI may help explore structure, continuity, and direction, but does not dictate content or decisions.

Why Author Mode exists

Without an explicit authorship boundary, AI systems tend to:

  • rewrite rather than support
  • introduce voice drift
  • overstep into authorship
  • blur responsibility for the final work

Author Mode addresses this by defining the AI’s role before the first response. The result is assistance without takeover.
