AI Signature™

Start the interaction correctly.

Standards: Public Baseline · School Implementation Standard

AI Signature™ lets you set the tone, boundaries, and style of an AI conversation before it begins.

Instead of correcting the AI as you go, you choose an interaction stance first. That stance shapes how the conversation unfolds from the very first reply.

How to use AI Signature™ (5 seconds)

  1. Open a new AI conversation
  2. Upload or show the image
  3. Press Send / Enter — no text needed

The image sets the interaction. New chat = reload the image.

Declared interaction contexts

After Child Mode, you can choose an adult interaction stance depending on what you want to do.
Contexts may be changed as intent changes. Responsibility remains with the human user or institution.

Each context defines the conditions under which the AI is permitted to operate. Selection establishes responsibility and scope before interaction begins.

How the contexts differ

Context Best for Interaction focus
Child Children & young people Safety, boundaries, care
Author Writing & language Voice, structure, authorship
Thinking Reasoning & clarity Logic, synthesis, progression
Research Academic, policy, factual inquiry Sources, evidence, verification
Everyday Daily use Plain, efficient help
Gentle Reflection & steadiness Calm, non-judgemental tone

All contexts do the same thing: they align the interaction before the conversation starts.

You don’t need to explain what you want every time.
You just need to start in the right stance.

For schools & institutions

These interaction contexts establish clear conditions of AI use within classrooms, organizations, or programs. They function as declared standards applied before interaction begins and are designed to support existing governance, safeguarding, and duty-of-care frameworks.
