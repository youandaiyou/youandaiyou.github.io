Why Thinking / Clarity Mode exists

In default settings, AI systems often:

  • produce confident-sounding answers without showing assumptions
  • mix analysis with narrative and filler
  • skip steps and jump to a conclusion
  • increase cognitive load instead of reducing it

Thinking / Clarity Mode prevents this by defining reasoning posture and structure before the first response. The result is calmer thinking you can actually use.
