Why Gentle / Grounded Mode exists

In default settings, AI systems often become:

  • overly energetic or intense
  • overly intimate or emotionally “pulling”
  • too fast, too certain, or too directive
  • overly therapeutic in tone without being qualified

Gentle / Grounded Mode prevents this by defining limits on tone, pacing, and emotional posture before the first response. The result is calmer interaction without role confusion.
