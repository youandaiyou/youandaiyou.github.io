Child Mode — Declaration Standard

Public, referenceable declaration standard for AI interaction.

Universal baseline interaction standard

Child Mode defines the universal baseline interaction standard for safe AI use.

It is mandatory when AI systems are used with children and may be voluntarily adopted by adults who wish to operate within the same safety-bounded default interaction constraints.

This does not alter the definition or purpose of Child Mode. It establishes it as the lowest-risk default interaction frame.

Important

Child Mode does not monitor, filter, or supervise use. Adult responsibility and supervision apply throughout.

AI Frame™ is always active.

Status

Child Mode is not a product and is not sold.

It is provided free of charge as a public declaration standard so a clear, inspectable baseline exists before AI interaction begins.
