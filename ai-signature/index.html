What Is an AI Signature™

A governed way to define how an AI is allowed to behave before the first response.

AI Signature™ is an interaction framework that establishes boundaries, role, tone, and responsibility at the start of an AI conversation. It ensures that the AI responds within a declared context rather than default assumptions.

All AI Signature™ modes operate within an always-on governing layer called AI Frame™. AI Frame™ is not a mode, feature, or product — it is the baseline condition under which interaction occurs.

System rule

AI Frame™ is always active. All AI Signature™ modes operate within it.

AI Signature™ does not require software installation, plugins, accounts, or technical integration. It works across mainstream AI systems by making interaction constraints explicit before the AI responds.

What problem it addresses

Each new AI conversation begins without retained context. Without explicit guidance, the AI does not know:

  • who it is interacting with
  • what role it should assume
  • what boundaries apply
  • where responsibility lies

Many issues attributed to AI misuse — particularly in education and public settings — arise from this absence of declared context. AI Signature™ addresses this by defining interaction rules before the first response.

How it works

An AI Signature™ consists of:

  • a clearly named interaction mode
  • a small number of declarative constraints
  • a required acknowledgement phrase

The acknowledgement confirms that the AI has accepted the declared interaction rules. Once acknowledged, the conversation proceeds within that governed context.

AI Signatures™ may be pasted, uploaded as images, or presented visually where camera input is supported. No explanatory text is required.
