What Is an AI Signature?

A simple method for defining how an AI system should interact at the start of a conversation.

AI Signature is a method for clearly establishing interaction context when beginning a conversation with an AI system. It provides a short, standardised entry that defines boundaries, tone, and responsibility before the AI responds. This approach is particularly relevant in educational and sensitive settings, where clarity of roles and safeguards matter.

AI Signature does not require new software, platforms, accounts, or technical integration. It works with mainstream AI chat systems by making the interaction context explicit from the first message.

What problem it addresses

When a new AI conversation begins, prior context does not automatically carry over. As a result, the AI may not know:

  • who it is interacting with
  • what role it should adopt
  • what boundaries apply

Many issues associated with AI use in education and public settings arise from this lack of initial context, rather than from information itself. AI Signature addresses this by making the interaction context explicit at the start.

How it works

An AI Signature consists of:

  • a clearly named interaction mode
  • a small number of declarative constraints
  • a short acknowledgement phrase

The acknowledgement is requested before the interaction continues. Once acknowledged, the conversation proceeds within the defined context. The signature can be pasted into an AI chat, uploaded as text, or provided by photographing/scanning the text.

Personal use

Separate AI Signatures exist for adults who wish to personalise how AI interacts with them (for example, writers and professionals). These are optional and not required for safe or compliant AI use.

Child Mode (free) More modes

Each AI Signature is supplied in calibrated variants that are tested rather than edited. Modifying the text can change how the AI interprets the interaction, reduce reliability, or prevent acknowledgement from appearing.

Note: Some acknowledgement phrases and mode titles are intentionally maintained in English. These function as control phrases interpreted by AI systems and should not be translated, to ensure consistent behaviour when pasted, uploaded, or scanned.

What AI Signature is not

  • It does not monitor AI use.
  • It does not record conversations.
  • It does not filter content.
  • It does not replace adult judgement or responsibility.

AI Signature is a contextual interaction method: a practical way to start AI conversations clearly, safely, and predictably—especially where responsibility and safeguarding matter.
