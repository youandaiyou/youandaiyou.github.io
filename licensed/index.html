Licensed Implementations

1. Child Mode — Licensed Implementation Pack

For schools that need formal adoption, not just usage.

  • Regulator-ready wording pack (policies, staff guidance, parent notice)
  • Version-locked Child Mode declaration (legal stability)
  • School implementation checklist (governance, supervision, scope)
  • Audit-friendly language for insurers and regulators
  • Right to reference “Child Mode implemented” in documentation

Does not include AI configuration or software — wording + governance only.

2. Child + Author Mode (Supervised Creation)

For structured writing and creative tasks under adult control.

  • Child Mode (non-overridable baseline)
  • Author Mode permitted only within defined tasks
  • Explicit prohibition of emotional, persuasive, or moral framing
  • Clear distinction between “task assistance” and authorship
  • Classroom-safe declaration text for supervised use

Used for essays, summaries, rewriting, structured drafts.

3. Child + Research Mode (Bounded Inquiry)

For fact-finding and information lookup without guidance drift.

  • Child Mode baseline
  • Research Mode constrained to retrieval and neutral synthesis
  • No interpretation, advice, or framing
  • Citation-first expectation language
  • Staff guidance on acceptable vs non-acceptable queries

Designed for homework support and factual exploration.

4. School / District License

For formal, system-wide adoption.

  • All licensed modes (Child, Author, Research as selected)
  • Institution-wide wording rights
  • Internal staff training guidance (non-technical)
  • Annual version-lock updates
  • Regulator / insurer alignment language
  • Centralized declaration standard for all classrooms

Licensed per school or district tier.

5. Custom / Institutional Pack

For governments, NGOs, or large education groups.

  • Jurisdiction-specific wording
  • Translation (e.g. Spanish baseline)
  • Alignment with national guidance or ministry frameworks
  • White-label or co-branded documentation
  • Long-term version governance

Licensing applies to wording, declarations, and governance documentation only.
No software, AI configuration, or system access is provided.
