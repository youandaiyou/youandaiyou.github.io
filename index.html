On the Edge of Dreaming

A psychological, dream-state novel by Raymond Proost

On the Edge of Dreaming explores the inner world that appears between waking and sleep. Guided by the radiance, the warmth, and the nightmind, the book traces a slow, introspective journey toward the original self—the part of you that remains beneath memory, noise, and expectation.

Themes include:
the radiance • the warmth • the nightmind • the presence • the original self • symbolic inner landscapes • meditative emotional depth

A quiet, symbolic novel for readers who enjoy dreamlike introspection, emotional clarity, and psychological revelation.
