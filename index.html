Why this exists

Most AI drift begins before the first prompt—when clarity is not declared.

Most people focus on what AI can do.
YOU AND AI™ focuses on how interaction begins.

When clarity is missing, AI feels overwhelming.
When clarity is declared, AI becomes usable.

What YOU AND AI™ supports

  • Declaring role, limits, and intent before AI use
  • Reducing drift, overreach, and dependency
  • Keeping human judgment visible and central
  • Working with AI without losing balance or agency

What this is not

  • a personality
  • an authority
  • a belief system
  • a replacement for thinking

It is a pre-interaction standard — not a mindset.
